Mandarin Basics

Lesson 1: Greetings

  • 你好 (Nǐ hǎo) — Hello
  • 早上好 (Zǎoshang hǎo) — Good morning
  • 晚上好 (Wǎnshàng hǎo) — Good evening

Lesson 2: Common Phrases

  • 你好吗? (Nǐ hǎo ma?) — How are you?
  • 谢谢 (Xièxiè) — Thank you
  • 对不起 (Duìbuqǐ) — Excuse me / Sorry
