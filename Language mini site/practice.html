Practice Activities

Activity 1: Match the Greeting

Match the greeting with the correct language:

  • Hello
  • Hola
  • 你好
  • こんにちは
  • Kumusta

Answer key: English, Spanish, Mandarin, Japanese, Filipino

Activity 2: Translate Simple Phrases

Translate these phrases to English:

  • Gracias
  • 对不起
  • おはようございます
  • Paumanhin

Answer key: Thank you, Sorry, Good morning, Excuse me
