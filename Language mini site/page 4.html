Lesson 4 — Common Expressions

In this lesson, you’ll learn polite and useful everyday expressions for greeting, thanking, and asking politely in different languages.

Everyday Phrases

Thank youGracias (Spanish)
Excuse mePardon (French)
PleaseOnegaishimasu (Japanese)
Good nightMagandang gabi (Filipino)
GoodbyeArrivederci (Italian)

Practice Activity

1. Say “Please” in Japanese → Onegaishimasu

2. Say “Good night” in Filipino → Magandang gabi

3. Say “Goodbye” in Italian → Arrivederci
