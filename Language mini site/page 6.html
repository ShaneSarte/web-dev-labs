Lesson 6 — Travel & Directions

This lesson covers essential phrases to ask for directions, find transportation, and travel easily in a new country.

Key Phrases

Where is the train station?¿Dónde está la estación de tren? (Spanish)
How much is a ticket?Combien coûte un billet? (French)
I need a taxiWatashi wa takushī ga hitsuyō desu (Japanese)
Turn leftLumiko sa kaliwa (Filipino)
Go straight aheadVai dritto (Italian)
